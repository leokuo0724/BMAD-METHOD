# Knowledge Builder - Content Quality Guide

## Philosophy

Knowledge notes generated by Knowledge Builder prioritize **human understanding** over technical completeness. The goal is to help readers quickly grasp:

1. **WHY** this topic matters
2. **HOW** it works conceptually
3. **WHEN** to apply it
4. **WHAT** the tradeoffs are

## Content Structure

**Purpose**: Give readers the "so what?" in 3-4 sentences

**Format**:

- **WHAT**: One sentence describing the article topic
